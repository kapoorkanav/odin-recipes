Red Pasta

Description

Red pasta, a quintessential Italian favorite, is characterized by its vibrant tomato-based sauce. The rich marinara sauce is made by simmering ripe tomatoes with olive oil, garlic, onions, and Italian herbs like basil and oregano. This comforting dish is often paired with pasta types like spaghetti, penne, or fusilli, ensuring a delightful balance of flavors and textures. Red pasta is versatile, with variations including meat sauces or roasted vegetables for added depth. It’s a go-to recipe for its simplicity, deliciousness, and ability to please any crowd.

Ingredients

The ingredients are as follows:

  • Pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne)
  • Tomatoes (fresh or canned)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic and onions
  • Fresh basil and oregano
  • Red chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan cheese (for garnish)

Steps

The steps are as follows:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onions until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes or tomato sauce and season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce until well coated.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.
